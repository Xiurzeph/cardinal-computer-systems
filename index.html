Frequently Asked Questions

How does Affordable Engineering work?
Affordable engineering provides senior-level expertise on a part-time, retainer, or project basis. You get access to enterprise-level skills and experience without the cost and commitment of a full-time senior hire. It's perfect for companies that need occasional high-level technical guidance.
How do you price your projects?
Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of your needs. We offer flexible arrangements including monthly retainers for ongoing support, fixed-price projects for specific implementations, and hourly consulting for ad-hoc technical guidance. All engagements begin with a discovery consultation to ensure the right fit.
What size company do you typically work with?
We primarily work with mid-market companies (50-500 employees) who have outgrown basic IT support but don't need a full-time senior engineer. These organizations typically have complex technical needs but want to maintain cost efficiency.
