Frequently Asked Questions

What areas in Maryland do you serve?

We proudly serve clients throughout the DMV (D.C., Maryland, Virginia) region, Baltimore, and Southern Maryland. While we don't typically cover the Appalachian Mountains area, we can make exceptions for special requests. Just reach out to discuss your specific location!

How does remote support work?

Our remote support services are powered by JumpCloud, a highly secure and reliable platform. Once you initiate a session, we'll guide you through a simple process to grant us temporary, secure access to your system. This allows us to diagnose and resolve issues efficiently without an in-person visit, all while ensuring your data remains private and secure.

What types of devices and operating systems do you support?

We offer comprehensive support for a wide range of devices and operating systems, including Windows, Linux, and Android. For smart home systems, we specialize in Vivint and Ring integration and troubleshooting. If you have another system, just ask—we may still be able to help!

Do you offer on-site visits for services?

Yes, on-site visits are available when necessary, particularly for hardware diagnosis and repair or if your location is a secure site requiring physical presence. For any on-site service beyond a 15-mile radius from our base, a travel fee of 50 cents per mile applies.

What is the typical response time for support requests?

We aim for prompt service. For new inquiries, we typically respond within 24 hours. Clients on a maintenance plan receive priority assistance and faster response times, ensuring minimal downtime.

How can I prepare for a remote support session or consultation?

To ensure a smooth remote session or consultation, please ensure you have a stable internet connection and reliable power. If you're experiencing an issue, having any error messages or a brief description of the problem ready can also significantly speed up the troubleshooting process. For screen sharing, a good internet connection is essential.
