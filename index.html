Frequently Asked Questions

Are you familiar with medical software?
Yes. We act as the liaison between your practice and your EMR/EHR vendors (like eClinicalWorks, Epic, Athena, etc.), ensuring updates are handled correctly and technical issues are resolved quickly.

Absolutely. We sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs) with our healthcare clients and utilize HIPAA-compliant tools for remote access, documentation, and communication.
How does your pricing work for small practices?
We offer flat-rate monthly bundles so you have predictable costs. No surprise bills for calling the helpdesk. We also offer project-based pricing for specific compliance audits or office moves.
