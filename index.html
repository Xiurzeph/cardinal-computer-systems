Frequently Asked Questions

What is a 'Fractional' Engineer?
A fractional engineer provides senior-level expertise on a part-time, retainer, or project basis. You get access to enterprise-level skills and experience without the cost and commitment of a full-time senior hire. It's perfect for companies that need occasional high-level technical guidance.
What size company do you typically work with?
I primarily work with mid-market companies (50-500 employees) who have outgrown basic IT support but don't need a full-time senior engineer. These organizations typically have complex technical needs but want to maintain cost efficiency.
How do you price your projects?
Pricing depends on the scope and complexity of your needs. I offer flexible arrangements including monthly retainers for ongoing support, fixed-price projects for specific implementations, and hourly consulting for ad-hoc technical guidance. All engagements begin with a discovery consultation to ensure the right fit.
